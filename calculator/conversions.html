Ozone Generator Output Conversion

Calculate generator output from flow rate and ozone concentration.

Generator flow rate in liters / minute.
Ozone concentration in grams / cubic metres.

Formula: Output [g/h] = FlowRate [LPM] * 60.0 * 0.001 * Concentration [g/m3]

Ozone Concentration Conversion

Ozone Concentration in Air (g/m3 to PPM)

Note that 1 g/m3 = 1 mg/l = 1 μg/ml.

Formula: Concentration [PPM] = Concentration [g/m3] * 467

Ozone Concentration in Air (PPM to g/m3)

Note that 1 g/m3 = 1 mg/l = 1 μg/ml.

Formula: Concentration [g/m3] = Concentration [PPM] / 467

Ozone Concentration in Air by weight (g/m3 to % and PPM)

Note that 1 g/m3 = 1 mg/l = 1 μg/ml.

Formula: Concentration [%] = Concentration [g/m3] / 12.8
Concentration [PPM] = Concentration [g/m3] * 569

Ozone Concentration in Air by weight (PPM to g/m3 and %)

Formula: Concentration [%] = Concentration [PPM] / 7284
Concentration [g/m3] = Concentration [PPM] / 569
